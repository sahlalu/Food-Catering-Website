About Us

Founded on August 23, 1998, our catering service celebrates the essence of Indian occasions and Vedic culture. With 2000+ orders delivered, we've embraced tradition while empowering women and those in need through employment opportunities. Our mission is to elevate occasions through transcendent culinary experiences, driven by values of tradition, diversity, and excellence. We specialize in authentic Indian thalis, designed for weddings, corporate events, and more, uniting flavors that resonate with all. What sets us apart is our dedication to preserving culinary heritage and creating an experience that blends tradition with innovation.

Mission and Values

At Annapurna Caterers, our mission is to blend Indian culinary tradition with innovation, creating unforgettable experiences that celebrate diversity and uplift communities. Our values encompass authenticity, empowerment, excellence, inclusivity, innovation, customer-centricity, sustainability, community connection, passion, and cultural respect. We're dedicated to preserving the soul of tradition while embracing modern culinary creativity. Through locally sourced, organic ingredients, we minimize our footprint and maximize our positive impact.
